Homemade Lasagna

homemade lasagna recipes with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filing.
A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but this recipes calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan.
Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Prep Time :
1 hr 25 mins

Cook Time :
1 hr 15 mins

Additional Time :
10 mins

Total Time :
2 hrs 50 mins

Servings :
8

Yield :
19x13-inch lasagna

homemade lasagna Ingredients

This is what you need to make this top-rated homemade lasagna :

  • Meat: ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: A diced onion is cooked until translucent with the ground meat.
  • Canned tomatoes: can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs: chop 2 tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic
  • Sugar: A dash of sugar balance out all the acidity from the tomatoes
  • Spices and seasonings: dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Noodles: Lasagna noodle [use the oven-ready variety to save time]
  • Cheese: The cheese layer is made up of cottage cheese and Parmesan. Also need shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs: make the cheese layer extra creamy [and for holding the layer].

How to Make Homemade Lasagna

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
