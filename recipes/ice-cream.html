Homemade Peach Ice Cream

This peach ice cream is easy to make at home and the best you'll ever eat!

Prep Time :
15 mins

Additional Time :
4 hrs 20 mins

Total Time :
4 hrs 35 mins

Servings :
32

Yield :
1 gallon

Ingredients

  • 2½ pounds fressh peaches - peeled, pitted and chopped
  • 1 pint half-and-half cream
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoppon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whole milk, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2
    Working in batches, pur√©e peaches with half-and-half and sugar in a blender or food processor.

  3. Step 3
    Mix peach mixture, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla in a gallon ice cream freezer container. Pour enough whole milk into the container to reach the fill line, about 2 cups. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker.

  4. Step 4
    Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
